What is $CALC or the Calcio Network native token?

Calcio Network provides professional soccer players with blockchain based fan tokens to help them engage their audiences. $CALC is a fair launched token on BNB Chain with unique staking and farming pools serving as the exclusive Calcio Network platform currency. Holding $CALC will bring you extra benefits as unique NFTs, fan tokens, merch and many more.
